COMMERCIAL PROJECTS

Welcome to Picco Infra, your trusted partner in transforming commercial interiors. With over million's of square feet of commercial projects completed, we have the expertise and experience to deliver exceptional results across various sectors, including IT companies, the hospitality industry, small and mid-sized office spaces, and recreation clubs. Our commitment to quality, functionality, and aesthetics ensures that every project we undertake enhances the value and appeal of your commercial space.

Our Commercial Services

1. IT Companies

Create innovative and productive workspaces that inspire creativity and collaboration. Our services include:

  • Open Plan Offices: Design and layout solutions that promote open communication and teamwork.
  • Private Offices and Meeting Rooms: Stylish and functional spaces for focused work and meetings.
  • Breakout Areas: Comfortable and inviting areas for employees to relax and recharge.
  • High-Tech Infrastructure: Seamless integration of IT and AV solutions to support your business needs.
  • Ergonomic Furniture: Custom furniture solutions designed for comfort and productivity.

2. Hospitality Sector

Enhance the guest experience with our bespoke interior solutions for the hospitality industry. We offer:

  • Luxurious Lobbies: Elegant and welcoming entrance areas that set the tone for your establishment.
  • Guest Rooms and Suites: Beautifully designed rooms that offer comfort and style.
  • Restaurants and Bars: Ambiance-rich dining and drinking spaces that attract and delight guests.
  • Conference and Banquet Halls: Versatile spaces for events, meetings, and conferences.
  • Recreational Facilities: Well-designed fitness centers, spas, and pools that enhance guest satisfaction.

3. Small & Mid-Sized Office Spaces

Optimize your office environment to boost efficiency and morale. Our services include:

  • Space Planning: Efficient layout designs that maximize space utilization and functionality.
  • Reception Areas: Professional and inviting reception areas that create a great first impression.
  • Workstations and Cubicles: Custom-designed workstations that cater to different work styles and preferences.
  • Conference Rooms: Modern and well-equipped conference rooms for effective meetings and presentations.
  • Storage Solutions: Smart storage options that keep your office organized and clutter-free.

4. Recreation Clubs

Create vibrant and engaging recreational spaces that attract and retain members. We provide:

  • Clubhouses: Stylish and functional clubhouse interiors that serve as a central hub for members.
  • Fitness Centers: State-of-the-art gym and fitness facilities designed for optimal use.
  • Lounge Areas: Comfortable and inviting lounges for relaxation and socializing.
  • Game Rooms: Fun and engaging game rooms equipped with various recreational activities.
  • Event Spaces: Versatile event spaces that can be adapted for different occasions and gatherings.
