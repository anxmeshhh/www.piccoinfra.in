RESIDENTIAL PROJECTS

Welcome to Picco Infra, where we specialize in creating beautiful, functional, and personalized residential interiors. With a proven track record of completing interior works in over 100+ flats, individual villas, bungalows, and clubhouses, we bring unparalleled expertise and dedication to every project. Our team of skilled designers and craftsmen work closely with clients to turn their visions into reality, ensuring each space is both aesthetically pleasing and perfectly suited to their lifestyle.

Our Residential Services

1. Flats & Apartments

Transform your flat or apartment into a stylish and comfortable living space. Our services include:

  • Space Optimization: Smart design solutions to make the most of your space.
  • Custom Furniture: Tailor-made furniture that fits perfectly and enhances functionality.
  • Modern Kitchens: Sleek, efficient kitchens designed for both style and convenience.
  • Living Areas: Inviting living rooms that are perfect for relaxation and entertainment.
  • Bedrooms: Cozy and serene bedrooms designed for rest and rejuvenation.

2. Individual Villas

Create your dream home with our comprehensive villa interior services. We offer:

  • Bespoke Designs: Personalized designs that reflect your unique style and preferences.
  • Luxurious Living Spaces: Elegant living rooms, dining areas, and entertainment zones.
  • Gourmet Kitchens: Spacious and well-equipped kitchens that are a joy to cook in.
  • Opulent Bedrooms: Lavishly designed bedrooms for ultimate comfort and luxury.
  • Outdoor Areas: Beautifully landscaped gardens, patios, and terraces.

3. Bungalows

Enhance your bungalow with our specialized interior solutions. Our services include:

  • Classic and Contemporary Designs: Tailored to suit both traditional and modern aesthetics.
  • Functional Layouts: Thoughtfully designed spaces that maximize utility and flow.
  • Elegant Interiors: High-quality finishes and sophisticated decor elements.
  • Personalized Touches: Unique design elements that reflect your personality and taste.
  • Seamless Integration: Harmonious blending of indoor and outdoor spaces.

4. Clubhouses

Create inviting and luxurious clubhouses with our expert interior services. We provide:

  • Welcoming Ambiance: Warm and inviting interiors that make guests feel at home.
  • Versatile Spaces: Multi-functional areas for events, gatherings, and recreational activities.
  • High-End Finishes: Premium materials and finishes that exude quality and elegance.
  • Recreational Facilities: Stylish and functional spaces for fitness, entertainment, and relaxation.
  • Professional Designs: Designs that cater to the needs and preferences of club members.
