FURNITURE SOLUTIONS

At Picco Infra, we understand that furniture is more than just functional; it's an integral part of your space's aesthetic and comfort. We offer comprehensive furniture solutions, from custom-designed pieces to curated selections, ensuring every item perfectly complements your interior design vision. Whether for residential, commercial, or specialized spaces, our focus is on quality, durability, and style.

Our Furniture Services

1. Custom Furniture Design & Fabrication

Bring your unique ideas to life with our bespoke furniture services. We design and fabricate custom pieces tailored to your exact specifications, ensuring a perfect fit and unparalleled originality.

  • Tailor-made solutions for unique spaces and requirements.
  • High-quality materials and expert craftsmanship.
  • From concept sketches to final installation.

2. Office Furniture Solutions

Create productive and ergonomic workspaces with our range of office furniture. We provide solutions that enhance collaboration, comfort, and efficiency for any business environment.

  • Workstations, desks, and chairs designed for comfort and productivity.
  • Conference tables and meeting room setups.
  • Reception area furniture and lounge seating.
  • Storage solutions and filing systems.

3. Residential Furniture

Furnish your home with pieces that reflect your personal style and provide ultimate comfort. Our residential furniture services cover every room, from living areas to bedrooms.

  • Living room sets: sofas, armchairs, coffee tables.
  • Dining room furniture: tables, chairs, sideboards.
  • Bedroom furniture: beds, wardrobes, nightstands.
  • Custom cabinetry and built-in units.

4. Modular Furniture Systems

For flexibility and modern aesthetics, our modular furniture systems offer versatile solutions that can be reconfigured to adapt to changing needs and spaces.

  • Space-saving and multi-functional designs.
  • Easy to assemble and reconfigure.
  • Ideal for dynamic office environments and compact living spaces.

Why Choose Picco Infra for Furniture?

  • Design Expertise: Our designers create furniture that is both beautiful and highly functional, integrating seamlessly with your overall interior.
  • Quality Materials: We use only the finest materials to ensure durability, longevity, and a premium finish for every piece.
  • Customization: From dimensions to finishes, we offer extensive customization options to meet your specific needs and preferences.
  • Integrated Solutions: As a full-service infra solutions provider, we can offer furniture as part of a complete interior design project, ensuring harmony across all elements.
  • Client Satisfaction: Your satisfaction is our priority. We work closely with you from concept to delivery to ensure your furniture exceeds expectations.
