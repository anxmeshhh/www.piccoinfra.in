RENOVATION WORKS

At Picco Infra, we specialize in comprehensive renovation services designed to transform and revitalize your spaces. Whether it's a residential, commercial, or industrial property, our renovation works focus on enhancing functionality, aesthetics, and value. With a commitment to quality, efficiency, and client satisfaction, Picco Infra delivers exceptional results tailored to your specific needs. Here's an overview of the renovation works services we offer:

1. Reinstatement Works

Reinstatement works are essential for returning a property to its original condition, often required at the end of a lease or before a property handover. Our reinstatement services include: Space Clearing: Removing partitions, fixtures, and fittings installed during the tenancy. Repair and Restoration: Restoring walls, ceilings, and floors to their original condition, including patching, painting, and refinishing. Utility Disconnection: Safely disconnecting and removing electrical, plumbing, and HVAC installations added during the tenancy. Compliance with Regulations: Ensuring all reinstatement works meet local regulations and landlord requirements.

2. Protection & Hoarding Works

During renovation projects, it's crucial to protect existing structures and areas not undergoing renovation. Our protection and hoarding services ensure safety and minimize disruption: Protective Barriers: Installing temporary barriers and hoarding to protect existing structures, fixtures, and finishes from damage. Dust and Debris Control: Implementing measures to control dust and debris, ensuring a clean and safe environment during renovation. Safety Signage: Providing clear signage to guide workers and occupants, enhancing safety on-site. Noise Reduction: Implementing noise control measures to minimize disruption to adjacent areas and occupants.

3. Hacking Works

Hacking works involve the removal of existing structures, surfaces, and finishes to prepare for new installations. Picco Infra's hacking services include: Wall and Floor Hacking: Removing old tiles, concrete, and masonry to prepare surfaces for new installations. Structural Alterations: Demolishing non-load-bearing walls and partitions to reconfigure space layouts. Precision and Safety: Ensuring hacking works are carried out with precision and adherence to safety standards to avoid damage to structural elements.

4. Interior Finishing

Our interior finishing services focus on providing high-quality finishes that enhance the aesthetics and functionality of your spaces. These services include: Partition & Glass Works - Custom Partitions: Installing custom partitions to create functional and stylish spaces, including drywall, glass, and acoustic partitions. Glass Installations: Providing glass walls, doors, and partitions to enhance natural light and create an open, modern look. Ceiling Design Works - Suspended Ceilings: Installing suspended ceilings with various designs and materials to improve acoustics and aesthetics. Feature Ceilings: Creating unique ceiling designs, such as coffered, tray, or drop ceilings, to add architectural interest. Hacking & Tiling Works - Tile Removal: Carefully removing old tiles to prepare surfaces for new installations. Tile Installation: Installing a variety of tiles, including ceramic, porcelain, and natural stone, with precise alignment and finish.

Mechanical and electrical (M&E) works are critical components of any renovation project, ensuring the functionality and safety of building systems. Our M&E services include: Electrical Works - Wiring and Rewiring: Installing new electrical systems or upgrading existing ones to meet current standards and requirements. Lighting Solutions: Providing a range of lighting solutions, from general lighting to task and accent lighting, with energy-efficient options.
